Golden Harvest Scheme

At Avita Jewellery, we believe in making your dreams of owning exquisite gold jewellery a reality. Our exclusive Gold Scheme is designed to offer you a seamless and rewarding way to invest in gold. Whether you’re looking to purchase a timeless piece for yourself or a loved one, our scheme provides the perfect blend of flexibility, affordability, and luxury.

Term Duration

The Golden Harvest Scheme allows you to accumulate gold over a specified period. Here are the details:

  • Duration : The scheme typically runs for 12 months.
  • Monthly Contributions : You make regular monthly payments towards your gold savings.
  • Flexibility : You can choose a longer or shorter duration based on your preferences.

Benefits

  • Systematic Accumulation: Gold saving schemes allow you to accumulate gold over time through regular, manageable installments. Instead of a lump-sum payment, you contribute gradually, making it easier to afford that special piece of jewelry or build a gold nest egg.
  • Discounts on Making Charges: Most jewelers offer substantial discounts on making charges for gold purchased under these schemes. This means you pay less for the craftsmanship involved in creating your jewelry.
  • Flexibility : Many gold saving schemes provide additional services:
    • Lifelong Maintenance: Enjoy free maintenance for your jewelry even after the scheme matures.
    • Buy-Back Guarantee: Some schemes offer the option to sell back your accumulated gold at prevailing rates.
    • Insurance Coverage: You may receive free insurance coverage for a specific period, safeguarding your investment.
  • Fixed Gold Rate: When you enroll in a scheme, you lock in the gold rate at the time of joining. This protects you from market fluctuations during the accumulation period.

Scheme Conditions

  • Lock-In Periods:Most gold saving schemes have a predetermined lock-in period. During this time, you cannot redeem your gold. Be aware of this duration before enrolling.
  • Cancellation Charges: If you decide to exit the scheme prematurely, there might be penalties or charges. Read the terms carefully to understand the implications.
  • Redemption Rules :After the scheme matures, you can redeem your accumulated gold. However, each scheme may have specific rules regarding redemption:
  • Cash Refunds:Not all schemes allow cash refunds. Some may only offer gold or jewelry as redemption options. Ensure you’re comfortable with the redemption choices available.
